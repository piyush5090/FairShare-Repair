FairShare

Split bills, not friendships. Manage group expenses with total transparency and ease.

Overview

What is FairShare?

FairShare is a streamlined application designed to track shared expenses for trips, roommates, or events. We handle the math so you can focus on the memories.

Setup

Getting Started

Creating an Account

  1. Launch FairShare and tap Sign Up.
  2. Enter your name, email, and a secure password.
  3. Verify your account to unlock all features.

Logging In

Access your dashboard anytime by clicking Login and entering your credentials. We recommend setting up Biometric Login on mobile for faster access.

Groups

Managing Trips

Creating a Trip

  1. Tap the + New Trip button on your dashboard.
  2. Name your adventure (e.g., "Paris 2024").
  3. You'll automatically be set as the Trip Admin.

Adding Members

Navigate to the Members tab. Simply search for friends via their username or email to invite them instantly.

Expenses

Tracking Money

Any member can log an expense in real-time:

  1. Open your active trip and tap Add Expense.
  2. Add a description (e.g., "Sushi Dinner").
  3. Enter the total amount paid.
  4. FairShare logs the payer and prepares the split.
Finalize

Settling Up

When the journey ends, let FairShare do the heavy lifting:

  1. The Admin taps End Trip.
  2. Our algorithm calculates the optimized path for debt repayment (minimizing the number of transactions).
  3. View Suggested Payments (e.g., "Alex owes Sam $42").

Pro Tip: Add your UPI ID in your profile to receive instant transfers from your friends.
